# Code of Conduct

Spatula is a technical project, and participation here should stay respectful,
specific, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Keep criticism focused on code, docs, behavior, reproducible evidence, or project goals.
- Assume maintainers and contributors have limited time; make issues and pull requests actionable.
- Respect privacy. Do not publish private communications, credentials, personal data, or security details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized, discriminatory, or deliberately intimidating language.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after a maintainer asks you to stop.
- Public disclosure of security vulnerabilities before the project has had a reasonable chance to respond through [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues or pull requests, restrict repository access, or ban participants when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Report conduct concerns to `conduct@spatula.dev`. Report vulnerabilities through [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not through the conduct channel.
