<objective>
Close Gap 1 (Worker DI). Today `startWorker()` in `packages/queue/src/worker-entrypoint.ts` declares `let deps: WorkerDeps | undefined` and checks `if (!deps) throw new Error('WorkerDeps not initialized')` in every handler, but NEVER assigns `deps` — and the `_opts.deps` param is ignored. Every queued job DLQs. This plan builds a full `WorkerDeps` and assigns it, so the standalone worker image AND the embedded worker (`SPATULA_EMBEDDED_WORKER=1`) can process jobs.

Purpose: This is the foundational fix — Gaps 2 (per-job LLM config) and 3 (usage recording) both build on the deps + raw client constructed here.
Output: A new `buildWorkerDeps()` composition helper, an assignment in `startWorker()`, and a unit test proving fail-loud + non-null deps.
</objective>
